About Me

My Journey

My journey started with a simple goal: help students overcome their fear of mathematics.

Over time, I realized that most students struggle not because maths is difficult, but because the basics are not clear. With this understanding, I started teaching in a simple and practical way, focusing on:

  • Concepts: Clear understanding of topics
  • Practice: Regular exercises and homework
  • Real Examples: Math in daily life
  • Personal Attention: Small batches and flexible timing

My aim is to build confidence, improve problem-solving skills, and make learning an enjoyable experience for every student.
